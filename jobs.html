Civil Engineer

We have an immediate opening for a civil/stormwater engineer who can design and manage land development/stormwater engineering projects for a wide variety of projects in the area, as well as develop new clients for the firm.

Job requirements

  • B.S. degree in Civil Engineering
  • PA Professional Engineer license preferred
  • 7 to 10 years of experience in related site/civil land development and stormwater engineering
  • Philadelphia permitting experience and capable of supporting environmental projects a plus
  • Proven leadership capabilities and ability to function as a seller / doer
  • Demonstrated expertise in all aspects of land development engineering
  • Innovative problem-solving skills with ability to work independently with minimal supervision
  • Good interpersonal skills with ability to develop business, network and forge relationships with others in the industry
  • Good technical writing and verbal communication skills
  • Strong attention to detail with excellent quality control, and analytical and judgment capabilities

Technical skills

  • Zoning and ordinances reviews
  • Design of stormwater management and utility systems
  • Soil erosion & sediment control design
  • Preparation of construction cost estimates
  • Preparation of technical reports and proficient in the use of AutoCAD Civil 3D, Hydraflow, and Excel softwares

Trans-Pacific Engineering Corporation (TPEC) is a small but growing Civil/Environmental firm located in Willow Grove, PA with a wide client base throughout the region ranging from local homeowners to federal government agencies.

TPEC employees enjoy a friendly, flexible work environment and competitive salary with benefits package including paid holidays/flexible time off, health insurance, life and disability insurance, and Simple IRA with company match.
