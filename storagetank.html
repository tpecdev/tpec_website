storage tank management experience

Our staff has the skills and experience to assess your underground and aboveground storage tank problems. TPEC can select and conduct the appropriate testing programs, evaluate appropriate upgrading alternatives, provide design and construction supervision for tank removal and replacement, and assist you with operation and maintenance as well as apply for required permits.


Typical UST/AST services provided by TPEC include:


  • Preliminary Facility Review

  • Tank Integrity Testing

  • Site Investigation

  • Regulatory Compliance Evaluation

  • Tank Removal and Disposal

  • Continuing Environmental Activities

  • Replacement Tank Design

  • Construction Management

  • Program and Data Management

Mort Service Station, Philadelphia, PA

  • Provided oversight, confirmatory soil sampling and certification report for removal of 3 hydraulic lifts and a 275-gallon aboveground storage tank at an automobile service station.

  • Provided design of a 5,000-gallon sulfuric acid aboveground storage tank and a 5,000-gallon sodium hydroxide aboveground storage tank. Each tank was furnished with a concrete secondary containment, a transfer pump, a recirculation pump, and an overhead canopy to deflect rainwater.
