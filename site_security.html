site security experience

TPEC takes pride in working with the Philadelphia Water Department (PWD) on security system improvements to PWD's facilities for decades, such as the design and construction oversight of parking lots, roadways, stormwater collection systems, perimeter fencing, and communication and security system upgrades.

PWD Sampling Stations

TPEC has been working with Keystone Engineering Group to design sampling stations at various sites in the City of Philadelphia. The quality of drinking water is monitored by these sampling stations to ensure a safe and reliable supply of drinking water to all City residents.
