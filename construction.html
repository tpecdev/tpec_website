construction management experience

TPEC has the capability to provide construction management, construction inspection and final certification for all types of environmental projects. Our experience is invaluable to waste management operations and real estate development projects. By providing field construction services, TPEC completes the cycle from concept and design through construction and operations.


TPEC field services can carry real estate and environmental management projects from start up operations to provide a smooth transition to the client. These services are provided as support to our design function or as a third party verification for designs and plans produced by others. Our field services offer comprehensive support to our engineering design group. Various aspects of project development are provided below.


Typical construction management services provided by TPEC include:


  • Site layout

  • Field data collection and materials testing

  • Contractor/Owner liaison from bidding through selection and construction phases

  • Site evaluation and conditions testing and review

  • Material sample collection and conformance testing

  • Field construction planning and methodology

  • Environmental site cleanup and closure certification

  • Impoundments including liquid sumps, tanks and lagoons

  • Landfill liners, caps, infrastructure and site improvements

  • Erosion and sediment control systems

  • Containment and spill control structures

  • Earthen materials transport, handling, screening and placement

  • Compaction and permeability testing; laboratory and field

Confidential Client, New Brunswick, NJ

Managed removal of four 10,000-gallon USTs containing petroleum products at a truck rental facility. Managed installation of a soil and groundwater remediation system to address petroleum-related contamination.

Philadelphia Water Department,
Biosolids Recycling Center

Prepared a Request for Proposal (RFP) package for distribution to potential bidders, evaluated proposals from pre-qualified bidders, and reviewed submittals for design/build a 70,000 tons per year Thermal Drying Facility at the City of Philadelphia's Biosolids Recycling Center.


Superfund Site, Buena Borough, NJ

Prepared design plans and specifications and reviewed shop drawings for construction of a 500 CFM soil vapor extraction (SVE) system and a 500 GPM groundwater treatment system for removal of TCE and PCE from a Superfund site located in Buena Borough, New Jersey. The SVE system included a knockout pot, vacuum pump, and vapor-phase carbon absorbers. The groundwater treatment process included flow equalization, metals pretreatment, air stripping, and activated carbon adsorption.


Confidential Client, Harrison, NJ

Provided investigation, design, and construction supervision services for the implementation of an Environmental Cleanup Responsibility Act (ECRA) Plan which included installation of a composite cap, a stormwater collection system, a secondary containment system, and asphalt paving on a 5-acre site.


Envirite Corporation, Harvey, IL and Canton, OH

Provided design and construction supervision during modification of the containment system for twelve solid waste storage bunkers. The containment system consisted of steel and polyethylene liners, designed for compatibility with a wide variety of solid wastes while allowing installation and removal by an existing overhead crane.


Confidential Client, Indiana, PA

Provided turnkey services during design, permitting, installation, shakedown, startup, and operation of the groundwater remediation system for a site contaminated with chlorinated organics. The system was capable of remediating up to 20 gpm of contaminated groundwater and utilized pneumatically-operated groundwater recovery pumps. Groundwater treatment included particulate filtration, air stripping, and activated carbon adsorption.


Confidential Client, Yatesboro, PA

Provided design, construction oversight, shakedown, and startup of a groundwater remediation system for a site contaminated with chlorinated organics. The system was capable of remediating up to 20 gpm of contaminated groundwater and utilized electric submersible pumps. Groundwater treatment included particulate filtration, air stripping, and activated carbon adsorption.


Confidential Client, Garden City, NY

Provided resident engineering services during installation, shakedown, startup, and operation of a soil and groundwater remediation system at a former chemical storage and distribution facility. The system was designed to remediate up to 1,400 gpm of groundwater contaminated with chlorinated solvents and incorporated conventional pump-and-treat technology. A portion of the treated groundwater was used for in situ soil flushing, with the remainder of the treated groundwater being discharged to surface water through a permitted outfall.


Dutchess Landfill, NY

Supervised closure activities including capping, installation of synthetic liners, gas vents, leachate collection system, and stormwater cut-off and drainage system.

Preliminary Treatment Facilities - Prepared an operation and maintenance manual and conducted operator training. Sludge Processing Facilities - Conducted engineering studies for design of pressure filters and centrifuges, centrate pump stations, and chemical systems for sludge conditioning, including lime stabilization, ferric chloride addition, and acid wash. Provided design services for steel bulkheads, plant water systems, roads, service water systems, power distribution studies, and stormwater collection systems.


Hu-Kao Industrial Estates, Taiwan

Prepared pre-engineering investigation, detail design, and construction management for a 2 MGD wastewater treatment plant. The installed system incorporated extended aeration activated sludge processes. Prepared drawings and specifications for design of the Phase II expansion of 2 MGD wastewater treatment plant. Design activities included pre-engineering investigation, detail design, and construction management. The Phase II expansion included installation of three filter presses to replace the existing dry sand bed.
