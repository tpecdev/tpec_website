property condition assessment experience

TPEC has conducted numerous Property Condition Assessments (PCAs) for a wide variety of commercial and industrial sites. Properties inspected by TPEC include high- and mid-rise office buildings, flex buildings, industrial warehouses, hotels, apartment complexes, and shopping centers, with total building areas ranging from 20,000 to 1,000,000 square feet.


The general scope of PCA services provided by TPEC staff includes evaluation of site characteristics, building structure/architecture, mechanical, electrical and plumbing (MEP) systems, conveyance system, life safety system, fire protection system, and American Disabilities Act (ADA) Code compliance. The PCA is performed in accordance with ASTM standards.


TPEC has completed Property Condition Assessments for commercial and industrial properties nationwide. As a long-term consultant, we have completed over 100 projects for Aegon USA Realty Advisors, Inc., 60 projects for DRA Advisors, Inc., and 40 projects for Ramco-Gershenson, Inc.
