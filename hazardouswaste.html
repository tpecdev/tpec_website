hazardous waste management experience

Our staff is up-to-date with the latest regulatory requirements and governmental procedures in the management of hazardous and solid wastes. Our waste management specialists can provide a wide spectrum of engineering services, including waste stream inventory preparation, facility site selection, hydrogeologic assessment, feasibility and treatability studies, and remedial design. With our in-depth knowledge of waste management and state-of-the-art cleanup technologies, we can assist you with hazardous and solid waste related problems to achieve regulatory compliance.
