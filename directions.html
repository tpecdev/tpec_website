From a Specific Address


From Philadelphia International Airport

(approximately 45 minutes)

Follow the signs for 95 South - stay in the right lane. Take 476 North all the way to the Pennsylvania Turnpike, 276 East (extreme left lane). Leave the Turnpike at Exit 343, Willow Grove/Jenkintown. After going through the toll booth stay to your left. At the first light (Route 611 - Easton Road and Maryland Road) make a right. At the intersection of Maryland Road and Computer Avenue, make a left. Look for "Executive Mews" sign on your right. TPEC is located in Building "L", Suite 65 of the Executive Mews Office Complex.


From New Jersey Turnpike Exit 6

(approximately 40 minutes)

Take Exit 6 to the Pennsylvania Turnpike, 276 West. Leave the Turnpike at Exit 343, Willow Grove/Jenkintown. After going through the toll booth stay to your left. At the first light (Route 611 - Easton Road and Maryland Road) make a right. At the intersection of Maryland Road and Computer Avenue, make a left. Look for "Executive Mews" sign on your right. TPEC is located in Building "L", Suite 65 of the Executive Mews Office Complex.
